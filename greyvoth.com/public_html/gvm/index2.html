The World of GVM

Earth

A Survived Earth, 10,000 years after we're all gone.

Continents

Ottom

The Military Stronghold The land where all non-magic weapons are manufactured. All military arms are forged here. Ottom is known for its advanced weapons and vehicle technology. With huge weapons contracts with every other nation, it keeps the balance of war and peace.

Abya Yala

The Fight Kingdom The Robe family home. A country created by immigrants from all other countries who wanted to find their own way. Constant tournaments are held for money, fame and prestige instead of constant war. They have satellite stadiums all over the world and anyone can enter. The tournaments are held to find the strongest fighter on the planet.

Lune

The Home of the Wolves The land of natural riches. Lune’s infinite moon energy is other-worldly. The ancient wolves that settled here made sure that the land would always be rich in food, clean water and natural resources. This country does well in trade but al ot of the profits go to Lore and the AllKing.

Lore

The Country of Magic The Weber’s home country, It’s home to the biggest united army. Weber’s Lit Knights and Dover’s Eln Acolytes reside here. The birthplace of magic, it sets the rules and guidelines for all magical creatures, enchanted weapons and general magic usage around the world. Many of the towns in Lore magically float.

Hanor

Land of GOD Hanor is the God Dover’s land. He summons the AllKing once a year and demands tributes. This ritual is called God’s Call. This usually involves a sadistic act and human sacrifices.

Gradiis

Goth Country Gradiis is the land of the Goths and the only land the God's have no reign over. They are far away across a vast Ocean and very few have ever seen the land for themselves. They don't have any known magic but their land is rich in both warriors and resources.
