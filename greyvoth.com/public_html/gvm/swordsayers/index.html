SwordSayers Rules

The SwordSayers: Anyone who picks up a weapon or balls their fists to fight for the Gold and Glory. The Prizes if you are lucky enough to make it to the end are both promising and life altering. Enter all who are skilled enough to claim victory. Many deadly duels await.

  • 1. Entrance Requirement: Fighters must have achieved a significant level of prowess by either defeating a Final Boss or 100 Lord level opponents.
  • 2. Tier Equality: Fighters cannot exceed the tier of their opponent, unless the opponent has the capability to exceed it themselves. This ensures a fair and balanced match.
  • 3. Limited Lethality: Each fighter is permitted one kill per tournament. Any subsequent kills will result in immediate stoppage and inspection and may lead to disqualification. Kills do not include total eradication, extermination, or obliteration.
  • 4. No Holding Back: If a fighter purposefully holds back in their confrontation, neither participant will advance. This rule encourages full commitment and intensity in each bout.
  • 5. No Interference: Any interference during a bout will lead to an immediate stoppage and inspection and may lead to disqualification(s). The fight will restart once the commotion has been cleared.
